Our Workout

It’s rowing – and so much more. Alternating intervals on and off of the row machine deliver a triple threat workout: high-intensity sweat, low-impact burn, total body results.

Our Machines

Sexy row machines? Gorgeous and functional, our WaterRowers use water to create dynamic resistance. We also use the compact and easy to use PowerBlock dumbbells.

Each workout uses the latest Polar technology for fitness tracking and heart rate training. Monitor your effort during each workout and track the progress of your fitness journey.

We’re here to coach and motivate you to achieve dramatic results. Offering the personal attention and education of one-on-one training in a high energy group exercise class.
